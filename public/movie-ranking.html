Jun Kit's Favourite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time.

1. Arrival (2016)

Genre: Science Fiction
Director: Denis Villeneuve
Rating: 7.9

This is one of the most heart-wrenching sci-fi movie ever, totally not what it seems.

2. Suspiria (2018)

Genre: Horror
Director: Luca Guadagnino
Rating: 6.7

A remake of the 1977 horror film, although classic horror fans seems to hate it, but I like how it focus on more serious topic.

3. Confessions (2010)

Genre: Thriller
Director: Tetsuya Nakashima
Rating: 7.7

A mystery + revenge thriller based on a Japanese novel named Kokuhaku.
